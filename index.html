Hi, I'm Anjali! I'm a Scholar of Distinction at Barnard College of Columbia University, majoring in computer science. This past summer, I interned on the Google Ads Recommendations team as a STEP intern.

I've been exploring functional programming, open source software development, and programming language theory through my research and coursework. Currently, I'm a research assistant for Robot Perf, a vendor-agnostic, open source bench-marking suite designed to evaluate robotics computing performance. Last year, I worked on developing the SSLANG compiler using Haskell Stack as a research assistant for Professor Stephen Edwards.

In my free time, I love performing with Columbia Sur, Columbia's premier South Asian A Capella group, and working on my photography collection about Indo-Fijian heritage for my Barbara Horowitz '55 Scholars of Distinction Project.
